HOW TO MAKE TEA

Ingredients Required:

  • 2 cup water
  • 1 spoon tea powder
  • 2 spoon sugar
  • half cup milk
  • ginger
  • elaihi

Steps to make tea:

  • step 1:Boil the water.
  • step 2:Add tea powder,ginger,elaihi and sugar.
  • step 3:Wait for to boil it properly.
  • step 4:When it gets fully boiled add milk to the tea.
  • step 5:when it was fully prepared turn off the gas.
  • step 6:Enjoy your tea.
