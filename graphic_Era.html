GRAPHIC ERA UNIVERSITY

ABOUT GRAPHIC ERA:

Graphic Era Dehradun University is a private university located in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India. It was established in 1993 and is affiliated with the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields.

Here are some key features and information about Graphic Era Dehradun University:

    1. Academic Programs: The university offers programs in diverse fields such as Engineering, Management, Computer Applications, Commerce, Hospitality, Life Sciences, Humanities, and Social Sciences. It offers undergraduate (B.Tech, BBA, BCA, B.Com, etc.), postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA, MCA, M.Sc., etc.), and doctoral (Ph.D.) programs.
    2. Accreditation and Affiliation: Graphic Era Dehradun University is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and is affiliated with the AICTE. It has collaborations with various international universities and institutions for academic and research purposes.
    3. Infrastructure: The university has a modern campus equipped with state-of-the-art facilities. It includes well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, a library with a vast collection of books and online resources, sports facilities, hostels, and a cafeteria.
    4. Faculty: Graphic Era Dehradun University boasts a team of experienced and qualified faculty members who are experts in their respective fields. They are dedicated to providing quality education and guidance to the students.
    5. Research and Innovation: The university encourages research activities and provides opportunities for students and faculty to engage in research projects. It has research centers and collaborations with industries and research organizations to promote innovation and entrepreneurship.
    6. Placement Opportunities: Graphic Era Dehradun University has a dedicated placement cell that assists students in securing job opportunities. It maintains collaborations with reputed companies and organizes campus placements and job fairs.
    7. Extracurricular Activities: The university recognizes the importance of extracurricular activities and promotes sports, cultural events, and clubs for overall personality development of the students. It also hosts various seminars, workshops, and conferences.
    8. Rankings and Recognition: Graphic Era Dehradun University has received recognition and rankings from various organizations. It has been ranked among the top private universities in India by NIRF (National Institutional Ranking Framework) and other ranking agencies.
